About Us

Steel Authority of India Limited

The Steel Authority of India Limited (SAIL) is one of India's largest state-owned steel-making companies and is headquartered in New Delhi, India. Established in 1954, SAIL operates and owns five integrated steel plants at Bhilai, Rourkela, Durgapur, Bokaro, and Burnpur, along with three special steel plants at Salem, Durgapur, and Bhadravathi.
